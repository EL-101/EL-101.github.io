Richard: Artificial Intelligence in Education and Learning

The field of education and learning with artificial intelligence is a very diverse and evolving area. By using AI technology in this field, personalized learning experiences become possible. AI enables students to receive real-time education and serves as a tool for understanding and improving their learning ability. Understanding students' learning ability provides useful information for teachers and enables them to establish individualized teaching plans in the educational process. Additionally, AI can automate tasks in the educational environment, which improves the educational process and enables teachers to secure additional resources in their classes. Considering these factors, AI technology will play a crucial role in the field of education and learning.

Evon: Long term impacts of online learning

While surveys (reference) have shown that student satisfaction is higher while learning online and from home, studies suggest that the quality of learning, student engagement, and critical thinking has dropped and thus has negatively impacted students' education and learning outcomes. This is due to several reasons, students all come from diverse backgrounds that cannot be sufficiently supported entirely via online methods. These are some of the factors that contribute to the success of a student e.g.: access to resources outside of the classroom, technological knowledge (and how to navigate online systems provided by education providers), and other external commitments. Education providers tried to navigate and bridge the differences and gaps that students face with online learning, especially over the Covid-19 lockdowns however there is still a long way to go, and it is recommended that providers incorporate both traditional learning and online learning in a way that benefits all students while considering and supporting the other factors that greatly contribute to a student's success.
