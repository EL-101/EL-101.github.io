The field of education and learning with artificial intelligence is a very diverse and evolving area. By using AI technology in this field, personalized learning experiences become possible. AI enables students to receive real-time education and serves as a tool for understanding and improving their learning ability​

Understanding students' learning ability provides useful information for teachers and enables them to establish individualized teaching plans in the educational process. ​ Automation allows people to move away from mundane everyday tasks and duties and focus their attention on important and bigger projects – Making better use of our time​
