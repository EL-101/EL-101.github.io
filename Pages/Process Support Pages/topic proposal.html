Chosen Technology/Topic Name Topic Description and why it is important
Topic-name Artificial Intellegence in Education and Learning As a society, we have seen and experienced major events in the last several years that shaped how we currently operate on a day-to-day basis, with changes seen across all industries. Because of this, we believe that it is important to investigate and highlight how these changes have impacted students. What trends are we seeing already? Is AI a detriment or a useful tool for students? What are we seeing after pandemic and the shift to online learning? Lastly, what are some of the potential long-term impacts of online learning? Numerous studies and articles have started to investigate and discuss some of the impacts that these different topics have had on students so far.
